About KC Flowers

Hi, we are Kat Wojtowicz & Cheryl Pierce.Two highly experienced, dynamic, London based florists, with over 20 years experience between us.

Having freelanced together for 5 years, we decided to make our collaboration official creating our very own independent, London based, florist company for Weddings, events and everything in between.

With Kat’s Czechoslovakian heritage & Cheryl’s unique florist style we bring a fusion of interesting creativity. Whether its classic, contemporary, exotic or simple we work together to create bespoke floral arrangements.

We’ve worked on events and private functions such as Christmas insulation for Claridge’s, Sandra Rhodes, Mick Jagger, Rihanna & Peter Andre to name but a few.

We pride ourselves on creating a unique intimate experience for our customer’s. We can help to create the perfect floral arrangements to tell your love story on your big day, create celebratory floral displays or help you through the difficult process of choosing sympathy flowers for a loved one. We will be there every step of the way to guide you through your major event.
